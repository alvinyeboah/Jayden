Harmony Hall is our largest venue, accommodating up to 200 guests. It is equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual facilities and is suitable for weddings, conferences, and large-scale events.

Serenity Room provides a cozy and intimate setting, ideal for small workshops and team-building sessions. It can accommodate up to 50 people.
